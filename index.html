02Anatomy of an Intercept · Reference Case

How a Sovereign Sense Layer reads physical reality.

REFERENCE CASE Educational · Methodology illustration

On 15 April 2026, at approximately 11:05 pm, a fire broke out in the Alkylation unit of Viva Energy's Geelong refinery — one of only two remaining Australian refineries, producing ~10% of national fuel. Fire Rescue Victoria identified a mechanical or engineering failure as the likely cause, with operators detecting a pressure drop on control boards before the explosion. The 13-hour blaze resulted in approximately 150 million litres of diesel lost. We use this as a reference case to illustrate methodology only — no synARKa insight was published prior to this event.

SECTION 01
The Blind Spot

Why ground telemetry could not see what was coming.

Refineries rely on point-source sensors — physical gas sniffers, pressure gauges, control room telemetry — each reading one location. As a process unit drifts toward failure, the stress first shows up at facility scale: millimetric ground and structural deformation, and a persistent thermal shift across the affected unit's footprint — often before point sensors localise the cause.
✕Static ground sensors cannot triangulate thermodynamic drift at facility scale.
✕Data silos: terabytes of automated logs, no external structural lens reading the whole facility as one organism.
✕Maintenance optimised for production continuity, not pre-failure detection.
SECTION 02
The Orbital Discovery Flow

How commodity satellite data becomes structural truth.

A Sovereign Sense pipeline ingests SAR (Sentinel-1 free · ICEYE, Capella, Umbra on tasking) and thermal / multispectral (Landsat, ECOSTRESS, Maxar) for the facility coordinate boundary, then runs pixel-by-pixel change detection against a 6–12 month historical baseline, normalised against ambient weather to isolate genuine structural deviations.
→SAR / InSAR penetrates dust, cloud, darkness — millimetric ground and structural deformation (3–5 mm), the precision signal.
→LWIR flags persistent thermal anomalies at unit and asset-footprint scale (tens of metres per pixel) — zones, not single components.
→Change-detection logic flags persistent structural deviation against baseline.
→Anomaly cropped, validated via human-in-the-loop triage, alert dispatched.
